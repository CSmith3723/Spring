Welcome to PowerHousing

PowerHousing is a web application with the goal of putting power back in the hands of the consumer. Prospective renters or home buyers have the opportunity to quickly appraise median housing prices for various neighborhoods in the Tampa Bay area in comparison to their monthly income. PowerHousing aims to save consumers money and put decision-making agency back in their hands, especially when housing prices are high.
